Why MoneyMap IQ exists

Personal finance should not feel like rocket science.

People make big money decisions every day: buying a home, financing a car, paying down debt, saving for the future. Too often, the tools assume you already know what you are doing.

MoneyMap IQ is built for the moment before you feel confident. It gives you plain-English tools, clearer tradeoffs, and a practical next step.
