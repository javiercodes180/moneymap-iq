How do you want to save?

◇
Maximum safety

Cash guarantees stability with no risk of loss, but does not grow over time. Best for very short-term goals or when certainty is critical.

A HYSA or money market account can earn 3%–5% on average, helping your savings grow while still staying stable and accessible. It is a smart option if your timeline allows.

◇
Safer approach (recommended)

For short-term goals and large purchases, keeping your money in a high-yield savings or money market account helps protect your progress while still earning a modest return. Your money stays stable and accessible when you need it.

If your timeline is longer (3+ years) and you're comfortable with market ups and downs, investing could help your money grow faster — but comes with risk.

↗
Higher risk, higher potential return

Investing can grow your savings faster, but your balance may fluctuate. This can be risky for short-term goals.

For short-term goals, a HYSA or money market account offers stability and predictable returns without market risk.

HYSA (High-Yield Savings)
  • FDIC insured at banks
  • Usually has a fixed savings rate
  • Very stable and predictable
Money Market Account
  • Often bank or fund based
  • Yield can vary over time
  • May offer checks or debit access
