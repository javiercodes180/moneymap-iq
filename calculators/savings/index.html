Safer approach

For short-term goals and big life purchases, saving in cash, a high-yield savings account (HYSA), or a money market fund is typically recommended. Your money stays stable and available when you need it.

Not sure? For goals under ~3 years, most people stick with cash or HYSA.

Higher potential, more risk

Investing can help your savings grow faster, but values can fluctuate in the short term. This may make sense if your timeline is longer or you're comfortable with market ups and downs.

Not sure? For goals under ~3 years, most people stick with cash or HYSA.
