📋 What is PMI?

PMI stands for Private Mortgage Insurance. It's required by lenders when your down payment is less than 20% of the home price.

  • Typically costs 0.5–1% of the loan amount per year
  • Added to your monthly mortgage payment automatically
  • Can be removed once you reach 20% equity in your home
  • To avoid PMI, put down 20% or more
